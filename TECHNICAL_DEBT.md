## Explicitly not debt (deliberate tradeoffs, do not "fix")

These come up repeatedly in review because they look like bugs but are documented, intentional decisions — see `design/limitations.md` for the full list and `docs/decisions.md` for the rationale behind each:
- Regex-based extractors (salary/email/job-attributes) have false-negative gaps by design — a missed match is preferred over a wrong guess (AD-16, AD-20, AD-21, AD-22, AD-25).
- `notifications_log` retries indefinitely on failure with no backoff (see debt item #9 above for the one edge case worth eventually addressing — permanent failure, not transient).
- Single-user RLS model (`authenticated = full access`, no per-row ownership) — correct for this project's stated scope, not a security gap to "harden."
- Two independent, sometimes-disagreeing source-health signals shown side by side rather than merged (AD-18/AD-24) — a deliberate "fail visible" choice.
